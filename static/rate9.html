This app is a meal-prep calorie counter. If you make multiple meals in one go and/or eat the same meals repeatedly. This app will keep track of those meals for quick adding up.

I keep hearing MyFitnessPal is the best calorie counter, but the UI takes FOREVER to go anywhere. Theres way too much. Just take a look.

First you get your TDEE and I'll ask if your goals is to gain weight, lose it, or to maintain weight.
Then you'll swipe over to the explination. All you do is tap to write a new meal into existance.
The interface seems simple. Name your meal, and add calorie counts. I will add Macros such as Protien/Fat/Carbs. That isn't hard to implement. Just 3 text fields for that.
Now you'll see that I've added TWO meals. Tacos and Rice and Beans. These are two seperate meals. Maybe I had Tacos for Lunch, and will have tacos again tomarrow.

I'll tap on Tacos. It takes the count of 450 and add it to the total for the day.
Lets add Rice and Beans too!
To delete, just tap on the bottom left, and trash can icons will appear instead. The design allows for quick and easy deletion.
