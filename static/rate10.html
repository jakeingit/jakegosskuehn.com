I'm not sure if I can get away with this, selling phyisical items off the google play store apps, but if I could, I will.

This is a store that will feature one item within a time period, perhaps a day. The users can tap the item to see more details like shirt sizes, total cost, or whatever.

If I dont have anything in stock, I will link this item to an actual store and get commissions that way.
Tapping buy brings up a notification, linking you to a buy page. You'll complete a transaction, and that will send back a confirmation back into the application. I think adding all the sales into a database will be good enough.

If I get a sale, I will purchase all of the goods at the same time from the actual supplier, and then send them out a week later. I might do drop shipping.
The item is bought, the app updates based on inventory in the database. Lets check out the menu.
The menu brings you to a simple setting. It lets you toggle notifications, so you can know what is being sold that time period. You can also see all the purchases you had.
Another menu item will bring you to the vote page. You'll see future potential sales.

Each day, you will have one vote. For each item you buy, you get one extra vote each day.

So that will create a cascading illusion of many people voting and the people who use this app will benefit ALOT from it since they are chosing their own sales.
Once you cast a vote, you will see all of the votes for the listed items. I might have that number appear before or after all votes have been cast.
