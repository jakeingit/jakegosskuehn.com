Just like the DailyDealz mockup, this entire app is centered around a single penny auction interface. A user would buy IAP bids, and click their bids away in order to be the last person to have a bid before the time runs out. Each bid will reset the timer.

I have worked with the front and backend of penny auction sites. This will take a lot of funds to maintain. It will use some form of timing system like Cron Jobs in order to link it to a database that gets updated every single second...
Bidding resets the timer, casts you as the previous voter, and life is good.
Lets say no one else bids. You won it. It will lock the app-bidding for that item. I may have that screen hold for the rest of the day or prepare for the next entry in the penny stock deals.
Tapping on th menu, you can see your purchases and notifcation setting to have the deal sent to your phone. I'm not sure if google will like that one. If theres any ADS in a notification, then it will be bad news for me. But I'm not sure what the line is.
