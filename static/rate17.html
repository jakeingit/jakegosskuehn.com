The term clocking on Urban Dictionary brings us a result that says clocked is used in gay vernacular especially among drag queens to call out someone's flaws, to uncover or reveal the truth in a situation or one's true gender.

There are some communities for transmen and women that ask for feedback on how they appear. This app will be a safe spot for MtF and FtM people to post their pictures and have others 'clock' them if they pass. I will post 50% trans and 50% cis people. I will make a game about it. You're either right or wrong with highscores or something.

I want to do this to prove a point that if you are feeling any sort of isolation if you are trans that this is a safe space for it and share ideas. I will have links for proper attire guides, voice practing regiments, makeup infographics, and more.

I'm not 100% sure if the term 'clock' is a good idea here. I'm not sure if its completely used in a derogatory fashion. I'll ask some LGBT folk. It could be "Do I Pass" or something.

I also am doing this to know more about the LGBT community. I only know so much about the LGB part.
