"Studies have shown that creating or getting rid of a habit takes 30 days of consistancy". This app committs you to finish just ONE habit and integrate it into your life.

It would be pretty quick to implement this. The user would select a habit here.
After the habit, the user has to figure out how to solve the problem. How will they commit? I may have extra boxes for 'ideas' that the user can have. I list a few ideas here.
The interface is really simple. There will be notifications to make sure you've done it, and will nag you to do the ONE thing or to Avoid it for the day... You tap to complete, and increments it by day. If you do not complete it each and every day, it will think that you failed. Im not sure if I want to do a hard reset.
I may do a notification that says you fell off the wagon, but the user could have just forgot to log it in the app.
Top right menu in actionbar shows a menu. This will allow you to adjust your habit and your pathway to solving your issue.
