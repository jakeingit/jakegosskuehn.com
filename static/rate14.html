I tend to download a lot of things onto my phone off the Google Play store, however, Google Play doesnt give a quick way to add reviews after you download your app or game.

This app would work in the same way a Uninstall-All app would work. It finds all apps on your device, and you select to rate them.

Once you select all of them, tap the next arrow indicating that you're done picking who to rate.
Great. You selected all of your apps. You get an expanded list here. I think that selecting all of the apps you want to rate first, and then giving each a rating would allow for a faster UI flow and make the user happier. Hes not switching from choosing to rating, then to choosing. He can choose all, then rate all.
I may do a quick rate all 5 button, bc all developers want a 5, and most people are benevolent in their rating.

You can also add a title and description. I don't know if google will give you the option to do both off your device.
Once you click the next button, it gets the names of all of the apps you rated, and gives you a successful message. Then the app should take you to a success/THANK you page.
