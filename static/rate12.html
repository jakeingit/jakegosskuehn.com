Napolean is one of my favorite individuals in history. Considered one of the most charismatic leaders in the entire span of human existance, this guy knew what it takes to get around a social landscape.

Napolean would keep a notepad on him whenever he would meet people. He would write down each and every person he met and a few specific details that the average person wouldn't remember from their meeting.

Upon meeting them, they will be 100% astonished that you managed to remember each and every fine detail about their conversation. This is how you make friends.
You display interest in other people to gain their friendship. This is a tool to convey that interest.
You tap the floating action button to bring up a menu.

This will give you a new individual to track along with a image. These images will be a default set of images ranging from common icons to thumbnails of a girls face or old mans face to help you associate their name to an idea.
Now you have a list of all the people you're keeping track of now.

It says to tap to add notes. Simple, eh?
Tapping or pressing the action bar will bring up a dialog of what you want to log. Lets say we found out Amanda blushes with sushi. We should remember that.
Entering that in shows that Amanda has 1 Note.
Coming back to this app, you can see the note you left... You can cancel the note as well.
I suggest reading this app inspired love story. The list can scroll down for all the entires, and you could tap back on your device to see your list of individuals or tap the person icon on the bar to add a new one.
