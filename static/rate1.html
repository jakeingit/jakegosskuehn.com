  21. Have an app idea?
If you're like me. You might be an internet veteran. You could have hundreds or thousands of funny pictures that you have saved over time on your computer. They are nice to go back to, but they're basically collecting dust...
What I propose is that users who come to the site, upload their collection of memes, image macros, nsfw images, or whatever to help a meme-deprived person in need. In this example, its a hobo.
You start by making an account, and then you can upload an archive of files. Labeling when needed.
Once you upload, you'll get ImageBucks to spend on your character. Here you can give the character items and food to keep them alive. It will detract your funds to buy a new hat and pants... You can also buy new people to support.

Eventually you will have a huge collection of people you're supporting with your memes.
