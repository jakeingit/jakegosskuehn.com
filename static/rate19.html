This is a little self-incentivizing application that makes you feel like you've earned the right to treat yourself to something. If you feel like you just spend money to spend money or feel guilty whenever you think you don't deserve something, this app will help you get motivated and be glad you earned your right to buy yourself something nice.
You start by tapping to input a new goal reward. In this example, every time the user visits the gym, he will add 5$ to his reward money pool.
It adds the entries in a list from an internal database like Sugar ORM
Tapping tells the app that the user has earned a right to spend $5. An undo snackbar appears at the bottom.
A few more choices, I will add trash-can icons on the far right side to indicate that the user can trash the list item. He tapped and now has 55 bucks!
This person deserves something for his dedication. He went to the gym and read a book! Lets enjoy spending some of his money. Tap the box next to reward money to update the prize pool.
You've earned your new shoes user. Good job.
